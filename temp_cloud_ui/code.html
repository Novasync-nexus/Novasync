P R I S M A *

Prisma is a worldwide network of avant-garde visual creators. We redefine cinematic boundaries through technical precision and raw emotional storytelling.

I am Marcus Chen, a self-taught director. I have skills in color grading, visual effects, and narrative design.

For the past decade, I have obsessively explored the intersection of light and digital manipulation. Prisma was born from a desire to formalize this exploration into a cohesive studio practice. We don't just make films; we construct atmospheres that linger in the psyche.

Our approach is deeply collaborative and fiercely independent. By stripping away the bloat of traditional agency models, we maintain a direct line from inspiration to execution, ensuring that every frame remains true to its original intention.
